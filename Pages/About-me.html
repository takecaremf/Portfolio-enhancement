Mark Omega

(b. 1986, Abu Dhabi, U.A.E) is a project-based experimental sound artist. As a sound producer and technical director, he was a member of the curatorial team for new media of the Visayas Islands Visual Arts Exhibition and Conference (VIVA ExCon) 2018 in Capiz; and, technical coordinator for the Jejak-Tabi Exchange: Wandering Asian Contemporary Performances 2020 in Roxas. As a sound artist, he has performed in WSK X: Festival of the Recently Possible 2019 in Manila; and, participated in A Fly Enters/Immense Breath of the SEA: Residency in Open Contemporary Art Center-Taipei 2021. His current works delve into the field of noise, yet frequently drift into numerous sonic trajectories including free improvisation, dark industrial, drone, minimal, and environmental-ambient sound.
